* Concepts used

* Rest and APIs Refresher
-------------------------

Restful APIS ---> 
	. Using HTTP protocols to do transactions with a back end
	. Using GET, POST, PUT, and DELETE calls to the back end
	. Interacting with endpoints created on the back end
	. GET: gets the data	
	. POST: adds new data
	. PUT: updates data
	. DELETE: deletes data
